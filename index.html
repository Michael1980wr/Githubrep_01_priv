About

Engineering clarity
in complex logistics

I am a Senior Solution Designer with over 16 years of experience in automated integrated intralogistics. Based in Denmark, I design, calculate, and present large-scale warehouse automation solutions for clients across Scandinavia and Germany.

My work spans the full project lifecycle — from initial data analysis and concept design through to commercial negotiation and handover to project management. I have delivered solutions up to €50M in value as General Contractor in highly international environments.

Previously I spent seven years in Southeast Asia as Sales Manager and Project Engineer at SSI Schäfer, covering Singapore, Malaysia, India, Thailand, Indonesia, the Philippines, Taiwan and South Korea.
